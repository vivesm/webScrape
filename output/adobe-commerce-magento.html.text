Adobe Commerce - Powered by Magento
Adobe Commerce - Powered by Magento
(opens new window)
is an open-source e-commerce management platform that helps merchants and brands across B2C and B2B industries build eCommerce websites with customization and scalability. Use the trigger from the Magento/ Adobe Commerce connector on Workato to run the recipe each time a new event occurs in Adobe Commerce, and the actions to add customers and products, as well as search for customers, products, and orders.

How to connect to Magento on Workato
Workato uses token-based authentication to connect with Adobe Commerce. To establish a connection, provide your Adobe Commerce/ Magento login credentials including the server domain and account type (admin or customer), and click
Connect
.
Adobe Commerce/ Magento admin account allows you to manage your store, order, and customer data while a customer workspace allows you to see the site just as the customer does. In most cases, you will be connecting to your admin account.
