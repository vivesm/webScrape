#
AI features
Workato's AI features leverage advanced AI technologies to enhance various aspects of your automation processes. These features help you build, manage, and document your recipes more efficiently and integrate powerful AI capabilities into your workflows.
#
Getting started
To get started with Workato's AI features, you must meet the following requirements:
Your company has signed and agreed to Workato's AI feature addendum through DocuSign.
You are assigned the
Admin
(system role) in your workspace.
PERMISSIONS
Workato's AI features do not have granular permission settings. When you enable them in your workspace, all collaborators within your workspace can access them, regardless of their role.
#
Copilots
Workato's Copilots use AI to assist with various tasks in recipe building, optimization, and documentation. Each Copilot is specialized to streamline specific tasks and improve efficiency.
For more detailed information, refer to the
Copilots
documentation.
#
AI by Workato
Developed in collaboration with OpenAI, AI by Workato supports various AI-powered actions such as text analysis, categorization, email drafting, parsing, and translation. It enables you to implement AI capabilities without creating or fine-tuning prompts yourself.
For more detailed information, refer to the
AI by Workato
documentation.
#
Capabilities of AI features
Workato's AI features offer a range of capabilities to support various automation tasks:
Enhanced automation
AI features streamline automation processes by automating complex tasks, reducing the need for manual intervention, and increasing efficiency.
Data analysis
Analyze large volumes of data quickly and accurately, extracting meaningful insights to drive better decision-making.
Text processing
Perform text analysis, categorization, parsing, and translation, which simplify handling and interpreting unstructured data.
Intelligent suggestions
AI provides intelligent suggestions for various tasks, including generating emails, building recipes, and mapping data fields, saving time and improving accuracy.
AI BEST PRACTICES
Consider the following best practices to maximize the benefits of Workato's AI features:
Ensure your workspace meets the requirements and follow the steps to enable AI features correctly.
Review each AI-generated suggestion to ensure it aligns with your automation goals and requirements.
Test AI-generated suggestions thoroughly in a development or test environment to avoid issues in your production environment.
Ensure your data is clean and well-structured to achieve the best results from AI analysis and processing.
Continuously monitor AI-driven processes and make adjustments as needed to maintain optimal performance.
