Wait Until
This function card pauses the flow and waits until a specific date and time before resuming the flow.
The minimum wait time is one second and the maximum is 30 days. A value greater than 30 days results in the termination of the flow.
Input
Field
Definition
Type
Required
date & time
Specify the date and time to resume the flow.
This field accepts a value in ISO date format using UTC 
Z
 notation.

2016-09-07T16:55:25.670Z
