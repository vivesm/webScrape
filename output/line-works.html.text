#
LINE WORKS
LINE WORKS
(opens new window)
is a professional communication and collaboration platform that helps organizations connect, share information, and plan schedules efficiently. With features like instant messaging, shared calendars, task management, and file sharing, it suits businesses of all sizes and industries.
Workato's LINE WORKS connector enables integrations and automations within LINE WORKS, allowing you to automate tasks such as sending messages, searching user directories, retrieving bot lists, and creating channels. It also supports triggers for events like message exchanges, postback actions, and member participation in chats.
#
Prerequisites
Before using the LINE WORKS connector in Workato, ensure you have an active LINE WORKS administrator account with appropriate permissions to access APIs and permissions to use the Developer Console. Refer to LINE WORKS'
help documentation
(opens new window)
for detailed steps on creating an account.

Register a Bot in LINE WORKS
Configuring a bot in the LINE WORKS Developer Console is required to utilize this connector's triggers and actions.
Complete the following steps to configure a bot in the LINE WORKS Developer Console:
1
Sign in to the
LINE WORKS Developer Console
(opens new window)
using an administrator account.
2
Go to
API > Bot
3
Click
Register
.
4
Provide the
Bot Name
, a brief
Bot Description
, and complete the necessary fields.
LINE WORKS Register a Bot
5
Click
Save
to complete the bot's configuration.
6
Go to
Service > Bot
(opens new window)
.
7
Click
Add bot
to make the bot visible to members.
LINE WORKS Register a Bot
8
Locate the bot you registered in the previous steps and click
Add
in the
Add Bot
column to enable it for members.
9
Locate your bot and click its
Bot name
to open the
Bot info
pop-up.
LINE WORKS Register a Bot
10
Click
Edit
.
11
Click the
Visibility setting
toggle to make the bot public.
12
Click
Save
to complete the bot's configuration.
Refer to the LINE WORKS'
Bot Documentation
(opens new window)
for more details on bot configuration.

How to connect to LINE WORKS
Workato supports Oauth2 authentication to connect to LINE WORKS. This authentication method requires the following value:
1
Click
Create > Connection
.
2
Search for and select
LINE WORKS
as your connection on the
New connection
page.
3
Provide a name for your connection in the
Connection name
field.
LINE WORKS Connection
4
Enter the
Client ID
generated for your application in the LINE WORKS Developer Console.
5
Provide the
Client Secret
generated for your application in the LINE WORKS Console.
6
Select the necessary
OAuth scopes
(opens new window)
required for your integration. If no scopes are selected, the minimum set of required scopes will be automatically applied.
7
Click
Connect
to complete the connection.
