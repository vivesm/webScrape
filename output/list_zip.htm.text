Take two lists of equal length and combine them into a new list with paired items of the same position. 
For example, if you combined 
[1,2,3]
 and 
["a","b","c"]
, this function card returns a new list: 
[[1,"a"],[2,"b"],[3,"c"]]
. The input lists can be lists of any type.
