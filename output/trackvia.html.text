#
TrackVia
TrackVia
(opens new window)
is a low-code, cloud-based platform that streamlines workflows and enables companies to manage data, processes, and collaboration in a single environment.
#
API version
The TrackVia connector uses the
TrackVia OpenAPI
(opens new window)
.
#
How to connect to TrackVia
The TrackVia connector uses OAuth 2.0 authentication to connect to TrackVia.
Complete the following steps to set up your TrackVia connection:
1
Enter a
Connection name
that identifies which TrackVia instance Workato is connected to.
2
Enter your
TrackVia subdomain
. Go to
My Account > Advanced Customization > Custom Subdomain
in TrackVia to locate your subdomain. Leave this field blank to use the default subdomain
go.trackvia.com
.
3
Enter your TrackVia
Account ID
. Go to
My Account > Account Details > Account Info
in TrackVia to locate your
Account ID
.
4
Click
Connect
to open the
TrackVia Public API
authorization dialog.
Complete the connection fields and click
Connect
.
5
Sign in to your TrackVia account if prompted. If already signed in, proceed to the next step.
6
Click
AUTHORIZE
to grant Workato permissions and complete the connection to TrackVia.
Click
AUTHORIZE
to complete Workato's connection to TrackVia.
